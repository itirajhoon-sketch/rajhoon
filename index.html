Welcome to Govt ITI Rajhoon

The Government Industrial Training Institute Rajhoon was notified vide notification No. EDN(TE)A(1)27/2018 dated 24/05/2019. Four trades each two units namely Electrician, Fitter, Mechanic Motor Vehicle and Fashion Design & Technology have been approved in this institute. Out of above three trades namely Electrician, Fitter, and Fashion Design & Technology had been started in the campus of Govt. Middle School Peera since August 2019 session and Mechanic Motor Vehicle since August 2020 session (one unit each).
